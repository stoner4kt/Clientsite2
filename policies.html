Shipping Policy

At SGELAR, we ensure your school essentials reach you safely across South Africa.

  • Delivery Areas: We deliver to all physical addresses within South Africa (No PO Boxes).
  • Timeline: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 3-5 working days.
  • Costs: Flat rate of R100 nationwide. Free Shipping on orders over R1500 or 3+ pairs.
  • Tracking: You will receive a tracking number via email once dispatched.

Returns & Refund Policy

We take pride in our "Built to Last" promise. If you aren't satisfied, we are here to help.

  • 30-Day Returns: Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase in original packaging and unworn condition.
  • Exchanges: We offer one free size exchange per order. Contact us to arrange a courier swap.
  • Manufacturing Warranty: We offer a 6-month guarantee against stitching failures or sole separation under normal school wear.
  • Refunds: Processed within 7-10 working days back to your original payment method.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is protected under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

  • Data Collection: We only collect information needed to process your order (Name, Address, Email).
  • Security: We do not store credit card details. All payments are processed via secure encrypted gateways.
  • Third Parties: Your data is only shared with our courier partners to facilitate delivery. We never sell your data.
